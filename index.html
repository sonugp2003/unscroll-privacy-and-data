‚ôø Accessibility Service Transparency

Unscroll uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect short-form video content. Here's exactly what it does:

What the Accessibility Service Monitors:

  • Only 6 specific apps: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter/X
  • Detects UI elements indicating short-form video screens
  • No monitoring of any other apps on your device

What the Accessibility Service Does:

  • Performs back navigation when short-form content is detected
  • Updates local blocking counter (stored only on your device)
  • Nothing else - no recording, no data collection, no transmission

What the Accessibility Service Does NOT Do:

  • ‚ùå Does NOT record or capture screen content
  • ‚ùå Does NOT read text, passwords, or messages
  • ‚ùå Does NOT transmit data to external servers
  • ‚ùå Does NOT track activity outside the 6 supported apps
  • ‚ùå Does NOT access clipboard or keyboard input
